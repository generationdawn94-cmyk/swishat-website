— Short Bio (50 words)

Swisha T is an Ottawa-born rapper with 244K monthly listeners whose music lives at the collision of hard-hitting trap production and unflinching lyricism. Known for collaborations with 2Scratch and Pressa, his sound has built a significant following across Germany and Canada.

— Artist Bio

From Ottawa, Canada, Swisha T has built a reputation on the authenticity of his craft. Combining the gritty energy of trap production with deeply personal lyricism, his music resonates with listeners across continents — particularly in Germany, where his fanbase runs deepest.

Known for hard-hitting tracks like "NO LOVE," "Sober" (with 2Scratch and Pressa), and "Chapo," Swisha T's catalog reflects an artist unafraid to explore the full weight of his experience. His collaborative approach has produced some of the most-streamed moments of his career, and his return in 2026 marks a deliberate new chapter — sharper, more intentional, and built to last.

With 7 new songs and a completed music video, the next era of Swisha T is ready.
